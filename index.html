Features

Everything You Need Abroad, In Your Pocket

WanderVox is a travel companion app that combines real-time translation in 49 languages, camera-based text recognition, cultural guidance for 51 destinations, AI-curated local recommendations, and a dated trip planner.

How Does Voice Translation Work?

Voice Translation is instant speech-to-speech translation — tap, speak in your language, and hear the translation spoken aloud in theirs.

Tap to record, get instant voice-to-voice translation. Have real conversations with locals, negotiate at markets, or ask for directions - all without language barriers.

49 languages Two-way Natural voice output

What Can Smart Camera Translate?

Smart Camera uses AI to detect and translate menus, signs, and documents from a single photo.

Point your camera at any sign, restaurant menu, or document. WanderVox instantly detects the language, translates the text, and adds helpful context so nothing gets lost.

Camera & gallery Auto-detect language Contextual notes

What Cultural Tips Do You Get?

Cultural Guide delivers local phrases, do's and don'ts, gestures, and food guides for 51 destinations — cached offline so you can access them anywhere.

Learn essential local phrases, etiquette tips, and cultural insights before you arrive. Avoid awkward mistakes and show respect for local customs — cached offline for easy access.

Local phrases Etiquette tips Offline access

How Does Explore Find Hidden Gems?

Explore uses AI to surface hyper-local recommendations — street food stalls, secret viewpoints, and neighbourhood nightlife that most tourists never discover.

Discover what tourists miss. AI-curated local recommendations surface the best street food, hidden gems, nightlife, art, nature spots, and places to unwind - tailored to where you are.

Street food Hidden gems Nightlife & art Nature

What Does the Trip Planner Do?

Trip Planner turns your bookings, places, and reminders into one dated itinerary.

Add flights, hotels, restaurants, and activities. WanderVox groups them by date, converts currencies live, and reminds you before each pickup or check-in. Saved places sit alongside your bookings on the same screen.

Dated itinerary Currency converter Booking reminders Saved places

Does WanderVox Work Offline?

Android users can translate typed text fully offline via on-device ML Kit.

Download a language once and translate text without a signal — perfect for flights, the metro, or coverage dead zones. Voice and camera translation still need a connection.

Android On-device ML Kit No signal needed Typed text

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about WanderVox features, availability, and pricing.

How many languages and destinations does WanderVox support?

WanderVox supports voice-to-voice translation in 49 languages and curated cultural content for 51 destinations across Europe, Asia, the Middle East, Africa, and the Americas.

Is WanderVox free to use?

Yes! WanderVox is free with 5 voice translations and 3 camera translations per day. Need more? Grab a Trip Pass for $2.99/week, or go Monthly ($6.99/mo) or Annual ($49.99/yr) for unlimited everything.

Can WanderVox translate signs and menus from photos?

Yes. Point your camera at any sign, menu, document, or printed text and WanderVox will instantly detect the language and translate it into your preferred language with contextual notes.

What platforms is WanderVox available on?

WanderVox is currently available on Android via Google Play. An iOS version is in development and coming soon.

How does the Explore feature work?

Explore uses AI to curate personalized local recommendations based on your location and interests. It surfaces street food, hidden gems, nightlife, art, nature spots, and relaxation options that most tourists never find.

Can I plan my whole trip in WanderVox?

Yes. The Trip tab gives you a dated itinerary for flights, hotels, restaurants, and activities, with local booking reminders, live currency conversion, and saved places grouped into one screen.

Do I need to create an account?

No. WanderVox starts instantly as a guest using Firebase anonymous auth. Google Sign-In is optional — it keeps your identity if you reinstall the app. Trip data lives on your device.

Does offline translation really work?

On Android, typed text translation works fully offline using on-device ML Kit once a language is downloaded. Voice translation and camera translation still require a connection.
