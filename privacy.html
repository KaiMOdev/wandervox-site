Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 7, 2026

WanderVox ("we", "our", or "the app") is a mobile travel companion that provides voice translation, photo translation, cultural guides, and AI-curated local recommendations. Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the choices available to you.

1. Information We Collect

WanderVox does not require an account, login, or signup. We collect only the minimum data necessary to provide our services:

  • Audio recordings — When you use voice translation, we capture audio from your device microphone. This audio is sent to our backend server for processing and is not stored permanently.
  • Camera and photos — When you use photo translation, we capture images from your device camera. These images are sent to our backend server for processing and are not stored permanently.
  • Approximate device location — If you grant GPS permission, we use your approximate location to provide relevant cultural recommendations and local suggestions. Location data is not stored on our servers.

We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other personal identifiers. No user account is required to use WanderVox.

If you purchase a paid plan (Trip Pass at $2.99/week, Monthly at $6.99/month, or Annual at $49.99/year), payment is processed by Google Play. We do not receive or store your payment card details.

2. How We Use Your Data

All data we collect is used solely to deliver the features you use:

  • Audio recordings are processed to provide real-time voice translation between languages.
  • Photos are processed to translate text captured by your camera, such as signs, menus, and documents.
  • Location data is used to surface cultural tips and local recommendations relevant to where you are traveling.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to delivering the app's core features.

3. Data Processing and Third Parties

To power our translation and recommendation features, we use the following third-party services:

  • Google Gemini API — Audio recordings and photos are sent to Google's Gemini API for translation processing. Data is processed ephemerally and is not retained by our systems after the response is returned. Google's processing of this data is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Firebase — Used for app infrastructure and backend services. Firebase is operated by Google and is subject to Google's privacy terms.
  • Expo / EAS — Used for app delivery and over-the-air updates. Expo may collect basic device and crash information as part of its update service.

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

4. Data Retention

WanderVox is designed around ephemeral data processing:

  • Audio recordings and photos are transmitted to our server, forwarded to the Gemini API for processing, and discarded once the translation result is returned. They are not stored permanently on our servers or in any database.
  • Location data is used in real time to provide recommendations and is not persisted on our servers.
  • Because no user accounts exist, there are no stored user profiles, history logs, or saved preferences on our servers.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect data during transmission and processing:

  • All data sent between your device and our servers is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS.
  • Communication between our servers and third-party APIs (Google Gemini, Firebase) is also encrypted.
  • Since we do not store audio, photo, or location data permanently, the risk of a data breach involving your content is minimized.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access and deletion — Because we do not store personal data permanently or maintain user accounts, there is no stored data to access or delete. If you have specific concerns, contact us at the email below.
  • Withdraw permissions — You can revoke microphone, camera, or location permissions at any time through your device settings. The app will continue to function, though features requiring those permissions will be unavailable.
  • Opt out — You can stop all data processing simply by closing or uninstalling the app.

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or another jurisdiction with specific data protection laws, and you believe we hold any personal data about you, please contact us and we will respond within the timeframe required by applicable law.

7. Children's Privacy

WanderVox is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will take steps to remove such information promptly.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal, operational, or regulatory reasons. When we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this privacy policy or our data practices, you can reach us at:
